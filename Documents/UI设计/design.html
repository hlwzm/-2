📐 布局规范

画布: 1920x1080 (ScaleWithScreenSize, match=0.5)

层级: BgLayer(-10) → PanelLayer(0) → PopupLayer(10) → TopLayer(20)

间距: 大区块间距 30px, 内部间距 15px, 元素间距 10px

📦 数据接口

所有面板通过管理器 GameManager.Instance 的子管理器访问数据：

面板管理器关键方法
ShopPanelShopManagerGetShopList(category), BuyItem(id, count), Recharge(amount), GetMonthlyCard()
ChatPanelChatManagerSendChat(channel, msg), GetHistory(channel, count), SendPrivate(target, msg)
TradePanelTradeManagerSearchItems(keyword, category), SellItem(itemId, price), BuyItem(listingId), GetMyListings(), ClaimGold()
BagPanelGameManager.Heroes / PlayerPlayer data: gold, items list
QuestPanelQuestManagerGetQuestList(type), AcceptQuest(id), SubmitQuest(id), GetAchievements()
FriendPanelFriendManagerGetFriendList(), SearchPlayer(name), AddFriend(id), RemoveFriend(id), GetRequests()
GuildPanelGuildManager (via FriendManager)GetGuildInfo(), SearchGuild(name), ApplyGuild(id), GetMembers(), CreateGuild(name)
